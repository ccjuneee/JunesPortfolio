About Me

Hello, I'm June

I have been a Graphic Designer for many years, mainly in traditional print media. And while I still love that work, now is the digital age and, as such, I have begun to explore the world of interactive media.

I have strengthened my web and interactive skills and now offer more flexible design opportunities. My background in print, combined with new digital skills, lets me bring a holistic framework to every project — spanning graphic design, UX/UI design, and motion design.

Outside of work I enjoy drawing, reading, crafts, playing badminton, and spending time with my family.
